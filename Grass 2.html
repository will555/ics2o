Connecting to the Internet With a Already Established network

  1. Turn on your computer
  2. Go to the settings app
  3. Navigate to network and internet
  4. In the wi-fi section you will see the networks available
  5. Click your internet name
  6. Enter your password
  7. You will now have internet connection

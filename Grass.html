Installing Apps

Installing Steam

  1. Turn on the computer and monitor
  2. Open the internet browser
  3. Search for steam on Internet Explorer
  4. Go to the steam website
  5. Click on download on the website and follow the steps provided

Installing Avast Antivirus

  1. Turn on the computer and monitor
  2. Open the internet browser
  3. Search for Avast on Internet Explorer
  4. Go to the Avast website
  5. Click Download Free Antivirus
  6. Follow the Three steps to download Avast
